About

Hello, I'm Kay.

I am a fellow human being whose interests include writing, art & design, technology, and life sciences, among others.

One of my greatest passions is to create, to breathe life into the seeds of thought that bloom in my mind.

This small slice of the Web is a portfolio of my past works, and will hopefully serve as a motivator for me to continue creating throughout my university education.

I love ideas—especially the crazy ones. If you would like to chat or have any questions, feel free to contact me at quietelysium@gmail.com, or find me elsewhere:

DeviantArt | Instagram | LinkedIn | Medium
