About

Hello, I'm Kay.

I am a medical student and fellow human being whose interests include art, creative writing, coding, nature, and personal development.

One of my greatest passions is to create, to breathe life into the seeds of thought that bloom in my mind. This slice of the Web is a portfolio of my past work, ranging from the artistic to the scientific.

I love ideas and good conversation! If you would like to chat, feel free to contact me at quietelysium@gmail.com, or find me elsewhere:

Projects

Leafy | 2020
Leafy is a virtual library for individuals to study together while apart. My friends and I developed the platform at an exciting, stressful 24-hour hackathon.

Student Spaceflight Experiments Program | 2014-2018
A high school research project that culminated in the launch of our experiment to the International Space Station. We studied how levels of an enzyme, acid sphingomyelinase, changed in microgravity to give us insight into how astronauts develop muscle atrophy. As one of my first forays into the research world, this project ignited my interest in space and technology.

Ugly | 2017
The largest art project I've worked on to date, Ugly is an Adobe Flash comic that tells a tale about everyday facades and conformity.

Personal website | 2017
I spent much of my childhood designing drag-and-drop and simple HTML & CSS websites. Years later, naturally, I created one that reflects who I am today.
